SALAD OF JERSEY ROYALS

1kg washed Jersey royals (or new potatoes)
100g mayo
1 bunch thinly sliced spring onions White wine vinegar
Dijon mustard

Put the potatoes in to cold, salted water and bring it to the boil over a medium heat. It is important not to put potatoes in to already boiling water as the outside will cook and turn to mush before the inside has even gotten hot.

When the water is boiling, cook the potatoes for around 10 minutes. Check theyâ€™re tender with a skewer or knife, they may need a little longer. Check every 3 minutes. When cooked, strain them, and leave to one side to cool.

When cool enough to handle, chop the larger ones in to bite-sized pieces. Leave any smaller ones whole.

Mix your spring onions with the mayo, a splash of white wine vinegar, and a generous spoon of mustard, and season with salt and black pepper. Mix this with your potatoes. Serve as a side to any of the other spring dishes.
