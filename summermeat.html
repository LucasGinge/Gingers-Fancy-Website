SUMMER MEAT RECIPES

GRILLED STEAK, SWEETCORN, AND GARLIC, OREGANO, CHILLI BUTTER

1 ear of corn per person
Chopped garlic
Fresh Oregano
Chilli

Bring a pan of salted water to the boil. Tear the leaves off your corn and slice the corn in half. Drop in to your water for 12 minutes and remove. Allow to cool.

Get your barbecue on. Grill the steak how you like it, and allow to rest while you finish the dish. When the corn has cooled, get it on the grill. Turn it just as the kernels are charring.

Get a frying pan moderately hot and melt a fat knob of butter. When it has melted, throw in the garlic for about a minute, followed by the fresh picked oregano, and as much chilli as you like.

When the corn is charred all over, toss it in the butter with some sea salt. Serve with your steak.
