WELCOME!

Welcome to my fancy website! I’m delighted to present to you a concise catalogue of simple, seasonal dishes, made with ingredients that are readily grown and available in the UK, all of which you can cook at home. The recipes also contain tips from myself, a pro chef of 10 years.

I won’t overload you with information, or suggest ingredients that are particularly difficult to get. The aim is to make seasonality accessible. I want the reader to have a look at the recipes available at any given time of year and to be able to quickly pick one that stands out. Then, after you’ve realised how easy and worthwhile it is to produce stunning, delicious food within the ethos of seasonality, I’ll see you back here for more!
