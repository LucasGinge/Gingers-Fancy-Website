AUTUMN FISH RECIPES

MONKFISH WITH SALSA VERDE

1 lamb rump per person
Garlic
Small tin anchovies
1 bunch each fresh parsley, basil, oregano

*Lightly cure* the monkfish for a few hours.

Pick the leaves from your herbs and chop roughly or blitz in a blender. Crush a clove or 2 of garlic with a handful of capers, and a small tin of anchovies. You can blend these or do it in a pestle. Mix the chopped herbs with the garlic mixture, a squeeze of lemon and a glug of olive oil.

Pat the monkfish dry, lightly coat in oil, and add to a hot frying pan, remembering to place them away from you so you donâ€™t splash hot oil over your hand. Cook for 3/4 minutes on each side, depending on the thickness. Serve the monkfish with a generous spoon of salsa verde over the top.
