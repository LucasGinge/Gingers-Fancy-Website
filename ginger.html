GINGER

I (Ginger) was a chef for 10 years before I became a developer.

I have previously worked in hotels, members’ clubs, brasseries, fine dining restaurants, gastro pubs, cafes, festivals… Pretty much everywhere. Along the way, I’ve learned a lot about the techniques that help get the most out of ingredients.

I’m a strong proponent of seasonality as it marries up with sustainability. Through my experience, I’ve developed a strong understanding of, and a passion for, simple, seasonal cooking. I hope this website gives a sense of that.

I’ve never been one for recipes so the quantities are often kind of vague! My kitchen brigade would often ask me “Chef, how much of this do I put in?” to which I’d usually respond “Just make it nice”. If there’s something in a recipe that you don’t like, leave it out. Or add something you think would work. And the recipes are written with the assumprion you have most of the basics like butter, salt etc. ❤
