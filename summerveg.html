SUMMER VEG RECIPES

BEAN SALAD

200g each trimmed green beans, runner beans, fresh borlotti beans
*Vinaigrette*

Bring a pan of salted water to the boil. Trim off any stems from your green beans and runner beans, making sure you pull away the string from the inside of the runner bean pod. Slice the green beans in half and chop the runner beans in to 4/5 pieces. Pod and shell the borlotti beans (you can use dried, just soak them the night before.)

Cook the borlotti beans until tender, before scooping them in to ice water. Cook the green/runners together in the same water for about 2 minutes, add them to the ice water.

Strain the beans and toss in the vinaigrette with lots of black pepper and sea salt.

STRAWBERRIES

This is the embodiement of simple, seasonal food.

Simply pick off the tops and slice your strawberries in half. Lightly coat in lemon juice and a dusting of sugar, and toss. Leave them in a colander above a bowl in the fridge for a few hours. The strawberries will macerate, the flavour will intensify, and you’ll have a beautiful juice in the bowl.

Mix the juice through crème fraiche/whipped cream. Spoon the strawberries over the cream/juice mixture.
