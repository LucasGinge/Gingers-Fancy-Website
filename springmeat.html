NEW SEASON LAMB RUMP WITH BROAD BEANS

1 lamb rump per person
Fresh broad beans, podded and shelled
Small bunch of chopped mint
*Jus*

Get a decent sized pan of salted water boiling. Get an oven proof frying pan smoking-hot. Coat the lamb in olive oil and season generously. Sear the lamb on all sides. When browned all over, turn the lamb so it is fat side down, then place the pan in a pre-heated 180C oven.

Get your jus in a saucepan and bring to a low heat. Drop in the mint and leave to the side.

Drop the broad beans in to the boiling water. After 2 minutes, strain them and add them to the jus.

After 10 minutes in the oven, your lamb will be cooked. Put the lamb portions on a chopping board and leave for five minutes. Slice them how you like. Any juices on the board, mix in with the broad bean and jus mixture, with a knob of butter.

Scoop some broad beans on to each plate. Put the sliced lamb on top, then scoop over remaining broad beans and jus. This goes beautifully with this *Jersey Royal salad*.
