WINTER MEAT RECIPES

PAN-ROASTED DUCK BREAST, JERUSALEM ARTICHOKE PUREE, CHESTNUTS

1 duck breast per person
150g Jerusalem artichokes per person
50g chestnuts per person
*Jus*

Peel and chop the artichokes. Place them in a saucepan and top with enough milk to cover. Bring to a gentle simmer and cook, covered, until tender. Add a knob of butter, blend, and season.

Trim any excess skin/bone from your duck breast. Score the skin lightly, and put in a frying pan skin-side down, with a little olive oil. Put the pan on a medium heat and turn your oven on at 200C. Keep the duck on the skin side and check every couple of minutes. After about 10 minutes, the skin should be golden brown and crisp. Flip the duck over, season, and place in the oven for 10 minutes.

When cooked, place the duck on a chopping board. Put the pan back on a medium heat. Add the chestnuts with a knob of butter. Sauté the chestnuts until they’re starting to crisp. Slice the duck breasts. Add the juices from the chopping board to the chestnuts with the jus. Serve a generous amount of the puree, with the sliced suck breast on top. Spoon over the chestnuts and jus.
