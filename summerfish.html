SARDINE BRUSCHETTE (for the purist, it's pronounced with a 'k', not a 'ch')

4 butterflied sardines (8 fillets) per person
A handful of cherry/baby tomatoes per person
Garlic
Ciabatta

A few hours before serving, cut your tomatoes in half through the equator and *lightly cure* and leave them in a colander over the sink.

Get a frying pan/griddle hot. Coat the sardines in olive oil and place skin-side down in the pan/griddle, remembering to place them away from you so you donâ€™t splash hot oil over your hand. Cook for a minute on each side.

Grill/toast slices of ciabatta to your liking, then cut a clove of garlic in half. Rub the sliced end of the garlic all over the toasted bread to give the toast a light garlic fragrance.

Scoop tomatoes on top of the bread, a good glug of olive oil, and the sardines on the top.
